About the Robo Rhinos

Robo Rhinos are part of FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), where students design, build, and program robots to compete in yearly challenges. Our team blends mechanical design, software, electronics, CAD, and strategy — and we have just as much focus on gracious professionalism, outreach, and inspiring younger students.

  • School: Novi Middle School • Novi, Michigan
  • Team Number: 11254
  • Programs: FTC, mentoring FLL/elementary STEM events
  • Focus: Engineering design process, safety, teamwork, community
